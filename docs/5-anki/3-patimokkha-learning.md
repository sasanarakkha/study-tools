# Pātimokkha memorization

Made for aiding the memorization of the Pātimokkha, based on the book "The Bhikkhu Pātimokkha - Word by Word Translation by Bhikkhu Ñāṇatusita". It consists of cut-up mp3 recordings with the voice of Bhante Ariyadhammika’s and English translations. It is also include Pubbakicca. The audio originates from this [video](https://youtu.be/M7mKBHOD-Zg). It is available for testing and [feedback](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1Z8Jjt0-E0HNX7ygABIzAcrChG23M3IOyoZGQ-EDRzXY/viewform?entry.957833742=P%C4%81timokkha%20learning&entry.438735500=docs/5-anki/3-patimokkha-learning.md).
