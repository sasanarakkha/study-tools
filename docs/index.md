# Welcome

Welcome to the SBS DhammaVinaya Learning Tools. 

This webpage provides structured study materials, Anki flashcard decks, and specialized dictionaries designed to support students and practitioners in mastering Pāḷi and studying early Buddhist texts. 

Whether you are following the SBS Pāḷi-English Recitations, studying the Pātimokkha, or participating in formal Pāḷi classes, these resources are here to support your practice.
