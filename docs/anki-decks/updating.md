# How to Update Your Decks

Usually, it’s sufficient to double-click on the downloaded `.apkg` file, and it will update in your Anki.

__! Important !__ Before doing anything, synchronize your collection across all your Anki devices. Go to **Tools > Preferences > Syncing** and enable "*On next sync force changes in one direction*". This will provide a secure backup on the Anki cloud in case of any issues.

Please ensure that your import settings match the recommended defaults to update existing notes while keeping your scheduling statistics intact.
