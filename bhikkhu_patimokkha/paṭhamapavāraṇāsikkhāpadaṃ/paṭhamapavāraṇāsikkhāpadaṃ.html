PC 35 paṭhamapavāraṇāsikkhāpadaṃ

yo pana bhikkhu bhuttāvī pavārito anatirittaṃ khādanīyaṃ vā bhojanīyaṃ vā khādeyya vā bhuñjeyya vā, pācittiyaṃ.

If any monk who has eaten [a meal], who has been invited [to take more and refused], should chew uncooked food or eat cooked food which is not left over, there is an offence requiring confession.\n
