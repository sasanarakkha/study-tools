SA 7 vihārakārasikkhāpadaṃ

mahallakaṃ pana bhikkhunā vihāraṃ kārayamānena sassāmikaṃ att'uddesaṃ bhikkhū abhinetabbā vatthudesanāya
tehi bhikkhūhi vatthu desetabbaṃ anārambhaṃ saparikkamanaṃ
